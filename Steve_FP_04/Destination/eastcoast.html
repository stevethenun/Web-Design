New York City

Start your journey in the city of New York. Explore the vibrant waterfront, visit famous landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Times Square, and experience the bustling atmosphere of the Big Apple.Marvel at the skyline from the deck of your boat as you cruise past iconic landmarks like the Empire State Building and Brooklyn Bridge. Dive into the city's diverse culinary scene, from gourmet food trucks to Michelin-starred restaurants.

Chesapeake Bay

Anchor in secluded coves surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for kayaking and wildlife spotting. Immerse yourself in the region's maritime history by visiting museums and historic lighthouses along the bay's shores. Cruise through the scenic Chesapeake Bay, the largest estuary in the United States.Discover charming waterfront towns, enjoy fresh seafood, and explore historic sites such as Annapolis and Jamestown.

Charleston

Experience the historic charm of Charleston, known for its cobblestone streets, antebellum architecture, and rich culture. Stroll along the historic waterfront Battery promenade and admire the elegant mansions overlooking the harbor. Explore the city's historic district, visit plantations, and indulge in Lowcountry cuisine.

Beaufort

Discover the quaint town of Beaufort, known for its maritime history, scenic waterfront, and southern hospitality. Explore the Crystal Coast's barrier islands, known for their pristine beaches and abundant marine life. Dive into Beaufort's maritime heritage at the North Carolina Maritime Museum, featuring exhibits on pirate lore and shipwrecks.

Savannah

Conclude your journey in my personal favorite place the picturesque city of Savannah, known for its oak-lined streets, historic squares, and vibrant arts scene. Explore the city's historic district, take a riverboat cruise along the Savannah River, and savor the local cuisine. Experience the city's vibrant arts scene with galleries showcasing local artists and live music performances in cozy venues throughout the historic district.
